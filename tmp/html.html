Nitro Spring Offer 2025

Bubbles
  • 3 days ago
  • Updated
Not yet followed by anyone

Freshen up your style with our limited-time Nitro promotion valid through March 18, 2025! Read below to learn more about eligibility and how to claim your exclusive spring themed avatar decoration.

Please make sure your Discord app is updated to the latest version!

Promotion Summary

Offer Details: Get an exclusive avatar decoration when you sign up for or upgrade to Nitro Monthly or Nitro Yearly by March 18, 2025. Choose one out of three avatar decorations to use with your active Nitro subscription.

Nitro Spring Offer Avatar Decorations: Shower Stroll, Afternoon Breeze, and Hoppy Day

Note: The deadline to claim your avatar decoration is April 17, 2025. After this date, if you haven’t made a selection, you’ll automatically receive the Shower Stroll avatar decoration within two weeks after the deadline to claim your avatar decoration ends.
  • Promotion also applies to upgrades from Nitro Basic and Nitro Classic to Nitro.
  • Available on Discord’s desktop, browser, and mobile apps.

Eligibility Conditions

  • Discord users who have not had a Nitro subscription within 10 days before signing up for this promotion
  • Discord users who do not have an active trial or discount offer available. 

Regional availability: This promotion is only available in certain regions. If you do not see the promotion offer in your app, you are not eligible for this promotion.

Deadline for Claiming Promo: Sign up for Nitro Monthly or Nitro Yearly by March 18, 2025 (5:00PM PT) to be eligible for this promotion. Select your promotional avatar decoration by April 17, 2025 (5:00PM PT)— otherwise, you'll automatically receive the Shower Stroll avatar decoration.

What is Discord Nitro?

Discord Nitro is our premium subscription that unlocks a world of perks for your account. Personalize and enhance your experience with popular perks like HD streaming, 500MB file uploads, profile and avatar customizations, and more:

  • Custom & Animated Emojis
  • Custom Profiles
  • HD Streaming
  • Discord App Color Themes
  • Special Shop Perks
  • More Nitro & Nitro Basic perks

How do I claim my exclusive avatar decoration?

Desktop/Browser

Step 1: Go to the Nitro tab

On the desktop or browser app, press the Direct Messages button in the top left corner and then select the Nitro tab. Alternatively, you can navigate to your User Settings > Nitro.

To access the Nitro Page on desktop: Select the Direct Messages button, then press the Nitro tab

Step 2: Subscribe to Nitro Monthly or Yearly 

Once you are on the Nitro page, select Claim Offer in the promotion banner and choose either the monthly or yearly plan for Nitro.

Promotion Banner with “Claim Offer” Button on Desktop Nitro Page

On the next screen, enter your credit card information and other payment details. Upon successful completion of the payment, your account will be upgraded to Nitro! 

NOTE: If you don't have any existing payment methods, you'll be prompted to enter your payment information.

  

Desktop Choose a Plan Window (Left) and Nitro Activated Window (Right)

Step 3: Choose Your Avatar Decoration 

1. After subscribing to Nitro, a Nitro Activated window will appear. Press Next to continue.

2. In the Choose Your Decoration window, select your preferred avatar decoration and press Confirm.

Desktop Choose Your Decoration Window 

If you prefer not to claim your avatar decoration immediately after subscribing to Nitro, you can select your exclusive avatar decoration later through the Nitro page > Claim Your Decoration card.

Claim Your Decoration Card in Nitro Page on Desktop

Step 4: How to Apply Your Avatar Decoration

1. Once you have claimed your exclusive avatar decoration, you can head over to your User Settings by pressing the cogwheel [] icon in the bottom left corner. 

2. Then, select Profiles > Change Decoration where you will find all of your decorations. 

3. While in the Change Decoration window, navigate to the Exclusive to Nitro section and select your avatar decoration and press Apply. 

4. Be sure to confirm your changes by pressing Save Changes and you should be all set!

Example of Hoppy Day avatar decoration in Change Decoration Window

Mobile

Step 1: Go to the Nitro tab

1. On the mobile app, tap on your avatar in the bottom-right corner. 

2. Select the Nitro button in the upper-right to navigate to the Nitro page. Alternatively, tap the cogwheel icon [] > Manage Nitro. 

 

To access the Nitro Page on mobile: Tap your avatar in the bottom-right (Left), then tap the Nitro button in the upper-right (Right)

Step 2: Subscribe to Nitro Monthly or Yearly

Once you are on the Nitro page, select Claim Offer in the promotional banner and choose either the monthly or yearly plan for Nitro.

Claim Offer Button in Your Mobile Nitro Page

On the next screen, enter your credit card information and other payment details.

Step 3: Receive Your Exclusive Avatar Decoration

Upon successful completion of the payment, your account will be upgraded to Nitro! 

1. After subscribing to Nitro, a Nitro Activated window will appear. Press Choose Decoration to continue.

Mobile Nitro Activated Window

2. In the Choose Your Decoration window, select your preferred avatar decoration and press Confirm.

Mobile Choose Your Decoration Window

If you prefer not to claim your avatar decoration immediately after subscribing to Nitro, you can select your exclusive avatar decoration later through Manage Nitro > Claim Your Avatar Decoration card.

Claim Your Decoration Card in Nitro Page on Mobile

Step 4: How to Apply Your Avatar Decoration

1. Once you have claimed your exclusive avatar decoration, head back to your profile by tapping your avatar > Edit Profile.

2. Then, tap the Avatar Decoration box, and navigate to the Exclusive to Nitro section to find your claimed avatar decoration. Select your avatar decoration and tap Apply > Save to confirm. 

3. Be sure to confirm your changes by pressing Save in the upper-right corner and you should be all set!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why don't I see this offer in my Discord app?

A: If you don't see any promotion details on your Nitro tab across the desktop, browser, or mobile apps, it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria.

Q: I’m a current Nitro member. Am I eligible to get an exclusive avatar decoration?

A: If you are a Nitro member prior to the start of this promotion, excluding Basic and Classic members, you are not eligible to receive an exclusive avatar decoration at this time. 

We are cooking up more exciting things for current Nitro members - so stay tuned! In the meantime, you can check out all of the current Nitro benefits, including discounts and Nitro exclusive items in the Shop, here. 

Q: Where can I find my new avatar decoration and how do I apply it?

A: On desktop or browser, you can head over to your User Settings > Profiles > Change Decoration > Exclusive to Nitro to find your claimed avatar decoration. While in the Change Decoration window, select the avatar decoration you would like to use and select Apply > Save Changes.  

On mobile, tap your avatar icon > Edit Profile > Avatar Decoration > Exclusive to Nitro to find your claimed avatar decoration. Select your avatar decoration and tap Apply > Save to confirm. 

You can learn more about avatar decorations here. 

Q: Do I need Nitro to use the avatar decoration I received in this promotion?

A: Yes, you will need an active Nitro membership to use the avatar decoration as it’s considered exclusive to Nitro.

Q: What happens to the avatar decoration from this promotion if I cancel my Nitro subscription?

A: Your claimed avatar decoration will remain in your account, but becomes unavailable for use until you re-subscribe to Nitro. 

Q: How do I cancel my Nitro subscription?

A: You can cancel anytime in Discord by visiting Settings>Subscriptions, for more information visit How to Cancel Your Nitro Subscription. 

Q: If I upgrade my subscription from Nitro Basic or Nitro Classic to Nitro, can I receive this promotion?

A: Yes, this promotion applies for upgrades from Nitro Basic or Classic to Nitro.

Q: I claimed and redeemed a different free trial for Discord Nitro already. Can I access this promo?

A: Unfortunately, if you are currently on a free trial or have been in the last 10 days before attempting to claim, you are not eligible for this promotion. Have no fear, there will be more of these down the road! 

Q: If I have Nitro credit in my account, can I apply it to this promo?

A: If you have credit in your account from a previous Nitro membership, this credit will be automatically applied to the purchase total when you check out.

If you have credit in your account that is associated with a different Nitro membership type (ex: Nitro Classic), it will not apply to this promotion.

Q: I see a notification saying "You're ineligible for this offer". Why?

A: If you encounter this notification, it is because you do not meet the eligibility criteria outlined above.

Q: I purchased a Nitro membership during the promotion period, but I’d like to request a refund. What are the steps?

A: Before requesting a refund, please review our Refund Policy to ensure that your purchase is eligible for a refund.

If you’d like to proceed, please send in a ticket specifying that you’d like a refund for your Nitro purchase to our support team here: dis.gd/billing and our agents will be happy to assist you.

Please note that if your refund is issued, you will lose any promotional value or discounts associated with the purchase.
